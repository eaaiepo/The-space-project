Based on your current location and speed you’ll be descending in Aarhus, Denmark.

I’ll assign you to explore The Space Aarhus.

It’s a nice non-profit organisation run by cosmic travellers, just like you.

They can help you meet other like-minded beings and integrate and feel at home in Aarhus.

The Space offers:

- Networking events
- Social and cultural events
- Talks
- Leisure activities

Online and offline, and all in English!

These amazing events related to well-being, business, education, and friendship-building are always organised by incredible Spacers. Physical events are held at Fo-byen Aarhus: Frederiksgade 78C in Aarhus.

On their social media channels they also provide tips on how to find a job, hot spots and fun things to do in Aarhus/Denmark, and how to learn Danish - amongst other things.

“The Space to be in Aarhus to meet new people, connect and grow!”

The Space exists to inspire, empower, and educate internationals in Aarhus to meet, connect and grow.

We aspire to be inclusive and always do our best to help everyone feel welcome and at home.

We believe in the importance of community .

Together, we can co-create the world we want to live in. Therefore, we encourage you to join forces with The Space to help bring our purpose to life.

So who are these Spacers?

Each member of this community proudly wears this title.

The Space is for everyone in Aarhus.

Business professionals, students, parents, expats - basically everyone that is interested in our content and events! We all speak English when we’re together, but that’s not everyone’s native language.

Just like the first people in outer Space, we are pioneers. Brave people that came to live in Aarhus and were bold enough to explore unknown paths and leave our comfort zone.
